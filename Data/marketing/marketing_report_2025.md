Marketing Report for FinSolve Technologies Inc. - 2025
Executive Summary:

The year 2025 represented a breakthrough period for FinSolve Technologies’ Marketing Department, driven by advanced AI integration, hyper-personalized campaigns, and aggressive global expansion. Marketing efforts resulted in a 24% increase in new customer acquisition, an 18% rise in brand visibility, and significant improvements in customer retention. By leveraging predictive analytics and automation, the department achieved higher efficiency and scalability, positioning FinSolve Technologies as a strong global fintech brand.

Year-Over-Year (YoY) Performance:

Marketing performance in 2025 showed accelerated growth across all critical metrics:

New Customer Acquisition: Increased by 24%, fueled by AI-powered targeting and expansion into Latin American and Middle Eastern markets.
Brand Awareness: Grew by 18%, supported by global campaigns, influencer collaborations, and thought leadership initiatives.
Customer Engagement: Improved by 9%, with personalized content, chatbot interactions, and omnichannel engagement strategies playing a key role.
